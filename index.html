I am currently a PhD candidate at the Bioinformatics Algorithms Research Laboratory (BAL) in the Department of Computer Science at HKU, under the supervision of Prof. Ruibang Luo. I received my Bachelor of Engineering and Master of Engineering from HIT. I work on developing algorithms and tools for analyzing genomics data, especially third-generation ONT long-read data, and applications at different medical practices. My research papers have been published in peer-reviewed journals, including Nature Computational Science, Briefings in Bioinformatics, and NAR Genomics and Bioinformatics.

Research Interests

Algorithms, Bioinformatics, Deep Learning, Precision Medicine, Metagenomics, Text Mining
